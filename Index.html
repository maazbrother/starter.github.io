Cars

Cars, also known as automobiles, are vehicles designed for the transportation of passengers, goods, or both. They typically consist of a chassis, engine, transmission system, suspension, wheels, and various other components that enable movement and control. The history of cars dates back to the late 19th century, with the invention of the internal combustion engine and the first motor vehicles by pioneers such as Karl Benz and Henry Ford. Since then, cars have evolved significantly, becoming more efficient, safer, and technologically advanced.
